定格

一个记录在无限时间里的永恒瞬间的装置。基于Blender建模，具有AR交互功能。 
An installation Record the eternal moment in infinite time. Based on blender and AR technique. 

灵感、想法和工作过程的描述、最终结果和反思： 

一开始，我决定记录光影的变化。但在这个过程中，我有了留住某个珍贵瞬间的想法。我选择以胶卷相机照相的方式记录这些瞬间。同时，科幻电影2001太空漫游给了我关于时间空间和维度的灵感，使我的作业最终呈现这样的效果。

Description consists of inspiration, description of your idea and work process, final result and your reflections：

At first, I wanted to record the changes of light and shadow, but in the process, I had the idea of retaining a moment, so I chose to record in the form of film photos. At the same time, the movie 2001 Space Odyssey has inspired me about dimensions and practice, making my work program what it is now.


材料/Material

胶卷、镭射纸、透明塑料板

Film strips, Laser paper, Transparent plastic board

技术/Technique

Blender建模、百度AI开放平台、AR

Blender, Baidu AI, AR

弯曲的蓝色代表流动的时间，胶卷使无数个瞬间定格。挑选其中最令我感到舒适的一刻打印到透明的塑料板上，在交错的时空中寻找个人安全区。

The curved blue plastics represents the flowing time, and the film makes countless moments freeze. Choose one of the most comfortable moments and print them on a transparent plastic sheet to find a personal safety area in the crisscross space and time.
